Delivery Information

Postal Delivery Options

Standard Delivery: Our standard delivery service takes between 3-5 business days. You will receive a tracking number once your package is shipped.

Express Delivery: For faster delivery, we offer an express service that takes 1-2 business days. This service includes priority handling and expedited shipping.

International Delivery: We also offer international delivery options. Delivery times vary depending on the destination, typically ranging from 7-14 business days. Customs and import duties may apply based on your location.

If you have any questions or need assistance with your delivery, please contact our customer service team at support@myshop.com.
